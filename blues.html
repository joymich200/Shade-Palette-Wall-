Blue Colour Shades

Loved around the world, blue is a timeless hue linked to iconic Grecian villages, delicate Chinese ceramics, and elegant French country homes. With shades ranging from navy and teal to turquoise and duck egg, blue paint can stir emotions and bring a touch of wanderlust to your space. Which shade will you choose?

Dark Blue Paint Ideas

Navy blue paint adds a touch of moody sophistication, while other deep blue tones bring calm and serenity—whether used boldly or subtly. Teal paint evokes ocean vibes, offering a sense of relaxation wherever you are. Go all in with dark blue on every wall, create a striking feature wall, or use it as an accent for a dramatic finish.
