Introduction
Blue evokes a sense of serenity, trust, and depth. Whether you're after a breezy sky-blue hue or a grounded navy accent, blue color mixtures bring both peace and sophistication to interior spaces.

üåä Why Use Blue?

Blue can soothe, focus, or energize depending on its tone. Lighter blues create airy, refreshing rooms, while darker blues give depth, stability, and elegance. Blue pairs beautifully with whites, greys, and metallics.

üè† Best Places to Use Blue Paint
