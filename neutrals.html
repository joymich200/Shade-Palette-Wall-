White & Neutral Colour Shades

Think all whites are the same? Think again. The white and neutral colour palette is more diverse than it seems—from bright, brilliant white for a clean, modern canvas to soft champagne and ivory tones that add subtle warmth and elegance. Don’t overlook classic cream, a timeless neutral that pairs perfectly with country-inspired interiors. So, which shade of white will you choose?



Dark Whites & Neutrals

Add warmth and comfort to your home with darker neutral tones. Consider taupe for a rich blend of grey and brown, or opt for beige or oatmeal for an earthy, grounded feel. Magnolia remains a classic favourite—especially in high-traffic areas where durability matters. Don’t hesitate to mix deeper neutrals with lighter shades to create a balanced and inviting space.
