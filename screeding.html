About Our Screeding Service

Our professional screeding service ensures smooth, even surfaces that provide the perfect foundation for painting, wallpapering, or tiling. Whether it's new construction or renovation, we prepare your walls with precision and skill.

We use top-quality screeding materials and advanced tools to guarantee excellent adhesion, a smooth finish, and long-lasting durability.

Why Choose Our Screeding?

  • Perfectly smooth walls that enhance final finishes
  • High-quality cementitious screed for durability
  • Quick, clean, and professional service

Our Process

  1. Site Inspection & Surface Cleaning
  2. Mixing and Application of Screed
  3. Skimming, Leveling, and Curing
  4. Final Sanding & Preparation for Finish

Frequently Asked Questions

What is wall screeding?

Wall screeding involves applying a layer of cement or plaster mixture to make walls smooth and even for finishing.

Can you paint directly on screeded walls?

Yes, once the screed has fully dried and been properly sanded, it provides a great base for paint or wallpaper.

How long does screeding take?

It depends on the surface area and conditions, but most projects are completed within 1–2 days.
