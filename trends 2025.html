Top Interior Color Trends for 2025

By Love Mustapha · May 25, 2025

It’s the year of bold contradictions: serene earth tones meet vibrant statement shades. Whether you’re repainting a single accent wall or planning a whole-house makeover, here are the color directions designers can’t stop talking about.

1. Serene Greens

From sage to olive, green continues its reign—evoking calm and a deep connection to nature. Pair it with light woods and creamy whites for a spa‑like retreat.

2. Jewel‑Tone Blues

Smoky sapphire and peacock teal add an elegant punch. They work wonders on built‑ins, kitchen islands, or powder‑room vanities.

“Blue is the only color which maintains its own character in all its tones—it will always stay blue.” — Raoul Dufy

3. Warm Clay Neutrals

Think terracotta, baked clay, and sun‑washed adobe. These hues bring cozy warmth and pair effortlessly with greenery and matte black accents.

4. Optimistic Yellows

Cheery yet sophisticated, muted mustard and buttery pastel yellows are making living spaces feel sunnier—without the glare.

5. Monochrome Statements

Tonal rooms—one color in varying saturations—create drama while staying cohesive. Our tip: experiment with texture: matte walls, velvet sofas, glossy ceramics.

Which of these hues speaks to you? If you’re ready for a refresh, our design team is here to help bring these trends to life.
