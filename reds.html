Dark Reds & Pinks

Dark red paint brings a sense of mature sophistication to any space—especially rich burgundy tones, which radiate luxury and elegance, making them perfect for the living room. For a more playful touch, candy red offers a fun pop of colour, ideal for accents and smaller areas. If you're after a warm, relaxed vibe, consider a dark dusky pink. With its subtle, sun-kissed feel, it can give your space a Mediterranean charm that feels like a holiday at home.
