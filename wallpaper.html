Frequently Asked Questions

Is wallpaper suitable for all types of walls?

Yes. As long as the surface is clean, smooth, and dry, wallpaper can be applied to cement, drywall, or even painted walls.

How long does wallpaper last?

With proper care, wallpaper can last up to 10–15 years without fading or peeling.

Can I remove or change my wallpaper later?

Yes, most modern wallpapers are removable and can be replaced easily with minimal wall damage.
